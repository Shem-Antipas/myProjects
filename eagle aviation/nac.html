About Nairobi Aviation College

For over 20 years now, Nairobi Aviation College has made tremendous steps in provision of A level training to majority of Kenyans by offering aviation related courses. Among successful programmes which are offered in our branches include Kenya National Exams Council courses (KNEC) on Aeronautical engineering, Business, IT and Economics.
  • Vision & Mission
  • Philosophy/Motto
  • Objectives

Vision & Mission

VISION- To be a center of academic and professional excellence both locally and internationally
MISSION- To produce market driven graduates who are conscious of and responsive to the dynamic challenges in the society

Philosophy/Motto

Philosophy- Every profession has the ability to empower
Motto- Profession is power

Objectives

1. To produce qualified graduates for individual development and self reliance
2. Empower people through effective training that will translate them to serve the community to meet the industrial goals of our country
3. To offer quality ,affordable education to the members of the society with high ethical standards and self discipline
4. To supplement government efforts in training and development of human resources
5. To become an institution of higher learning with international reputation in order to curb capital flight
6. To nature talents among the youths through co-curricula activities in a bid to preserve our culture and heritage
7. To make Nairobi Aviation College an institution with a difference

Nairobi Aviation history timeline

1999

Nairobi aviation college was astablished Mainly as a college for teaching aviation courses which included :Aeronautical Engineering,Aircraft operations,Travel and Tourism.The college was located at Nature House Tom Mboya Street

2002

Due to increasing demand of Hire Education in country ,Nairobi Aviation college Numbers grew drastically and the college was moved to  uchumi House Where by the college aquired a bigger space.During this year,other courses:Business studies,computer studies,Journalism and Hospitality where introduced.

2003

The college had its first ever graduation ceremony where 2000 plus graduands were celebrated.It is the same year when the college purchased its first bus to facilitate student activities like sports ,trips and drama

2006

Nairobi Aviation college football team was the best university and colleges team and was promoted to play in the super league which is a top tier division.Since then the team has been playing in the colleges and universities league.

2007

Due to the increased capacity and expansion of the academic courses ,Nairobi aviation partnered with Moi university and Busonga university from Uganda to be offering their programs.This partnership was a good me sign for college toward achieving university status

2009

The college was recognized and awarded as the best institution for IATA courses in africa.The was through merits based on the quality training and qualified tutors  which was reflected on students performance.

2012

The college acquired its own Aircraft for  training Aeronautical Engineering students.During the year,the college installed  a state of the art kitchen and a restaurant for the Hospitality students

2013

The college drama and music teams emerged the best country wide and got a chance to perform for the president at the state house.The college had grown to be a power house for these two activities

2018

The college the college moved to bank house which is one of the prime building along Moi Avenue.This was due to space and proximity of the building for our students

2020

Through years of academic experience,the college is well rounded in all the courses on offer. At moment is : still the best in Africa for offering IATA courses,  best in many music and drama categories which were held the year 2019,has state of the art kitchen and restaurant for hospitality students,state of the art media equipment,a power house for sports and also the students seamlessly enjoys campus life
